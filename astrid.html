Background

During an internship at Astrid Textiles—a B2B company specialising in exclusive fabrics—I explored the intersection of craftsmanship and commercial operations. The focus was on strengthening the brand’s digital presence while gaining deep insight into the unique dynamics of the textile industry.

Marketing & Brand

Worked hands-on with brand building and visual storytelling for Instagram and Pinterest, focusing on inspirational, value-driven communication suited to an exclusive B2B audience.

  • Contributed to communication planning aimed at long-term brand awareness.
  • Implemented pull marketing strategies to drive demand through inspiration rather than short-term promotional pressure.
Industry & Operations

Gained a practical understanding of B2B dynamics where material quality and craftsmanship are the primary drivers of demand. I supported day-to-day commercial operations to understand the full flow of a textile business.

  • Managed orders and inventory within Visma.
  • Built insights into logistics, supply chain flow, and warehouse management.
Analysis & Strategy

The internship culminated in a comprehensive business analysis of Astrid Textiles. This strategic reflection covered several key pillars of the organisation:

  • Leadership & Culture: Analysis of organisational structure and company values.
  • Financial & Systems: Review of financial structure and the business systems supporting growth.
  • Positioning: Reflections on the company’s competitive standing within the premium textile sector.
