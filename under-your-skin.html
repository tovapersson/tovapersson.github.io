Background

This project focused on how Under Your Skin could convert first-time buyers into loyal, returning customers through strategic CRO and UX improvements.

Customer insights showed that shoppers in the 45–65+ segment primarily purchase via mobile, value clarity and trust, and seek long-term self-care solutions. Behavioral analysis revealed significant friction, with major drop-offs indicating gaps in navigation, motivation, and perceived value.

Approach

UX & Funnel Optimisation

  • Identified friction points through behavioral data, recordings, and funnel analysis.
  • Simplified mobile navigation to reduce cognitive load.
  • Strengthened social proof and "before/after" clarity to support purchase confidence.

Loyalty & Retention Strategy

  • Positioned the loyalty club as the core retention driver, making membership more visible and easy to join.
  • Reduced login friction and streamlined membership flows.
  • Designed clearer value communication around points and community belonging.

Testing & Iteration

  • Validated hypotheses through A/B testing; transforming loyalty entry points into visual CTAs increased attention and registrations.
  • Applied learnings to hierarchy and emotional storytelling across the customer experience.
Results

The work established a clearer retention-focused customer journey where first purchases feel trustworthy and joining the loyalty program is a natural next step.

Together, these improvements strengthen the foundation for higher retention, stronger lifetime value, and a more community-driven brand experience.
