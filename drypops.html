Background

DryPops was a newly launched brand aiming to drive traffic to its website and generate sign-ups ahead of launch. Our task was to develop original creative concepts and ad copy, and to conduct A/B testing across different target audiences.

Approach

We created tailored messaging and visual concepts for two key audiences:

  • Young adults (18–29): Playful, colorful creatives focused on making tap water more fun.
  • Parents (30–45): Value-driven messaging emphasizing everyday convenience and functional benefits.
Results

The campaign generated measurable traffic and engagement, confirming the initial hypothesis of a strong product–audience fit among parents.

  • Total campaign: 223 link clicks, 0.92% CTR.
  • Parents: Highest performance with 202 clicks and 0.97% CTR.
Reflection

The results highlighted the importance of data-driven A/B testing to validate assumptions. Future optimization should focus on deeper segmentation and funnel strategy to convert sign-ups to customers.
