Background & Objective

The objective of this project was to build a Shopify-based e-commerce brand and reach SEK 100,000 in revenue without an initial media budget, while validating demand through data and ensuring profitability from day one using GM3-driven decision making.

Search trend analysis revealed a 174% increase in demand for “scarf” between 2022–2026, alongside limited competition in the mid-range segment—indicating a clear market opportunity.
The primary target audience became women aged 50–70: a financially strong yet underserved segment that values quality, transparency, and timeless style over fast trends.
Strategy

Product & Positioning

To enable a sustainable mid-range/premium positioning, cotton was selected over silk, providing the right balance between perceived quality, usability, and accessible pricing.

The assortment strategy combined:

  • Timeless hero products with stable demand
  • Customer co-creation for future collections
  • Data-driven demand insights connected to seasonality and use-cases

This reduced guesswork in product development while strengthening long-term customer relationships.

Profitability Framework – POAS & GM3

Performance marketing decisions were governed by two layers:

  • POAS as a directional efficiency metric for scaling channels
  • GM3 as the true profitability guardrail, ensuring each order contributes positively after marketing and variable costs

Scaling only occurred when both POAS and GM3 aligned, preventing growth without margin integrity.

Framework SEE-THINK-DO-CARE

SEE

Customer intention

“I care about style and want inspiration that feels timeless and relevant to me.”

Strategy

Build mental availability and emotional recognition through consistent visual identity, representation, and inspirational content.

Channels / KPIs

Organic & paid social, PR, and strategic event partnerships.
KPIs: Reach, engagement, traffic, and CTR.

THINK

Customer intention

“I want to understand if this product fits me and feel confident before deciding.”

Strategy

Reduce uncertainty by combining emotional reassurance (System 1) with rational proof points (System 2) such as product details, social proof, and trust signals.

Channels / KPIs

SEO/SEM, retargeting, and value-driven email communication.
KPIs: Time on page, scroll depth, add-to-cart rate, and funnel drop-off.

DO

Customer intention

“I want to complete my purchase easily and safely.”

Strategy

Eliminate friction in checkout through clear delivery/return terms, trusted payment methods, and simplified flows, while validating profitability via POAS and GM3.

Channels / KPIs

Search & Shopping, retargeting, email, and SMS.
KPIs: Conversion rate, CPA/CAC, AOV, checkout completion, POAS, and GM3.

CARE

Customer intention

“I want reassurance that I made the right choice and a reason to stay loyal.”

Strategy

Strengthen long-term relationships through personalization, loyalty programs, community, and co-creation, shifting future purchases from rational to habitual.

Channels / KPIs

CRM, email, SMS, and membership community.
KPIs: Repeat purchase rate, CLV, engagement, and referral rate.

Tech Stack & Infrastructure

The technical foundation was designed for fast launch, reliable measurement, and long-term scalability:

Commerce & Payments
  • Shopify as core e-commerce platform
  • Klarna, Apple Pay, and relevant local payment methods
Data & Analytics
  • GA4 for behavioral tracking and funnel analysis
  • Server-side–ready tracking and structured data layer
  • Looker Studio for cross-channel KPI visualization
CRM & Lifecycle Marketing
  • Klaviyo for segmentation, automations, and lifecycle communication
  • First-party data capture through membership, quizzes, and post-purchase flows
  • Personalization powered by behavioral and preference data
Acquisition & Feeds
  • Meta and Google Ads for demand capture and retargeting
  • Product feed optimization for channel-specific intent alignment

Key Takeaway

SUIE demonstrates how an e-commerce brand can be built without a large initial media budget by combining:

  • Data-validated demand
  • Clear audience positioning
  • GM3-controlled profitability
  • STDC-structured customer journey
  • First-party data as a long-term competitive advantage

The result is not just a launched store, but a scalable commercial system where growth, customer value, and profitability are structurally aligned.
